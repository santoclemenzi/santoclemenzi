### :computer: Small web apps I built for fun and learning :

- [Oggi in TV](https://oggiintv.vercel.app/) I like to see TV guide, even if I don't watch TV that much. I really like the TV guide format some smart TV have so I decided to replicate that format with this small Flask PWA.
- [CloudPlayer](https://cloudmusicplayer.vercel.app/) is the answer to a simple need: playing music from your various cloud accounts with a single player. PWA with just React and Tailwind CSS. No remote database, everything lives in your browser.
- [Digital Business Card](https://farmaciasantacroce.vercel.app/) started as a PoC and I think it's actually currently used. It's a small digital business card with a sharing feature through QR code and NFC. React, Tailwind CSS, Supabase.
- [CarBro](https://carbro.altervista.org/): I got the first idea for this app back in 2023, when I just needed a smal web app to track car services and expenses, as switching to iOS left me without free usable options (this is actually a long story, I used one app but I was sick of the ads popping up all the time). The challenge here was the hosting service: I had this very old free hosting service with php, 100MB of storage and free MySQL. I thought "let's do it in php with the least footprint possible". Well, this app has a size of about 1,5MB and it's blazing fast (and no ads, of course).
